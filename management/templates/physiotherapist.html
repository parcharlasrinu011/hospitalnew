What is Physiotherapy?
Physiotherapy is a degree-based health care profession in which skilled physiotherapists use their knowledge to improve a wide range of conditions involving different parts of the body. Physiotherapists treat people affected by illness, injury, or disability through manual therapy, exercise, and movement. A typical physiotherapy approach also includes educating the patient about their condition, restrictions, and strengths.

Physiotherapy is not limited to a particular age group and has proven beneficial for people of different demographic with different conditions. Physiotherapy plays a vital role in recovering from various geriatric conditions, neurological, neuro musculoskeletal, cardiovascular and respiratory, etc. Physiotherapy has also been proven beneficial in geriatric condition such as fall prevention etc. They help in pain management and facilitate recovery post surgical recovery helping people to bounce back to their regular routine as early as possible.
